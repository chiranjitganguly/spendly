Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed version of your password. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate and improve the Spendly service. Your expense data is used to generate the summaries and reports displayed to you within the app. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to your account. We apply standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We will only disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please contact us. We will action your request within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Spendly after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
